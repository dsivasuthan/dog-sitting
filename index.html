About Us

What we do

At Hachiko's we provide high quality, personalized, and professional care for all your doggie needs. We offer services in the Toronto, Leslieville, Scarborough, North York region.

We are a small business with caring and reliable trained staff whose motto is: "your dogs are our dogs." We always have longer adventures because we believe that dogs are wild at heart, and deserve to "run like the wind," ensuring their full happiness and complete exhaustion when they go home.

But what is Hachiko?

Our company's name is inspired by the story of Hachiko, the Japanese Akita, who's remarkable loyalty to his dead owner kept him waiting, in vain, at the Shibuya Train Station for 9 years. At this company, we understand that dogs aren't just animals but cherished and beloved members of your family, and we want to provide them with the best care and love when you're away.
